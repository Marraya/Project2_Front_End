Welcome to my shop, traveler.

My name is Mario and I'm a web designer. In this portfolio, I'm going for crappy 1998 aesthetic™ which I hope you will enjoy. A sublime blend of blandly focused elements coupled with a radioactive yellow seems about right.

How did I get here anyway? Vibrating through the cosmos, I guess I discovered that working via CMS's doesn't actually make you a good designer.

Ultimately aesthetic = function but it's difficult to express this. We find ways to create clarity amid the confusion but it often gets lost in translation. Too many details is the same as too many cooks.

Good design is complex; great design is simple.
